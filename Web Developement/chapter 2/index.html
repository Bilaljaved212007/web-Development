Plot

A child from the dying planet Krypton grows up on Earth until revealing his amazing abilities as he seeks acceptance in his new home. A wrathful Kryptonian military commander poses danger to planet Earth making Superman confront his destiny by becoming the protector of humanity

Key Information

Genre: Action, Mystery, Thriller, fictional

Created by: David S.Goyer

Release Date: June 13, 2013

Network: Netflix

Cast

Henry Cavill as Clark Kent / Superman, Amy Adams as Lois Lane, Michael Shannon as General Zod, Russell Crowe as Jor-El, Kevin Costner as Jonathan Kent, Diane Lane as Martha Kent

Characters

Superman (Henry Cavill): Alien hero protecting Earth, struggles with identity.

Lois Lane (Amy Adams): Brave reporter, discovers and supports Superman.

General Zod (Michael Shannon): Ruthless villain wants to rebuild Krypton on Earth.
